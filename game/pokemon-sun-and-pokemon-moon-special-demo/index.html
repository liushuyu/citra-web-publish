Summary

Pokémon Sun and Moon: Special Demo Version runs without problems in Citra. Graphically, the game doesn’t have issues, however you might experience some audio slowdowns, which might be caused by weaker hardware. There are no crashes or huge slowdown and there is no need to transfer save files from Citra to 3DS and vice versa.
