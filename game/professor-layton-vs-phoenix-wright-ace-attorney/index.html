Summary

Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney performs quite well on decent hardware, though slowdowns are expected. The game doesn’t have any graphical or sound issues with Citra. However, the DLC doesn’t currently work, even if you copy the extdata from a 3DS.
