YO-KAI WATCH 3

Rating
Okay
Game functions with major graphical or audio glitches, but game is playable from start to finish with workarounds.
Type
Game Icon
Title IDs

Yo-kai Watch 3 runs pretty smoothly on Citra, and it’s easily able to run at full speed. The only time it slows down or lags is when you use the Yo-Kai Watch to complete a quest or a part of the story to continue, or finding a Yo-Kai under a car or a tree. Here, the speed drops drastically.
